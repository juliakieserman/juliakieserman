about me

Oh the places you'll go!

I’m a software developer with a passion for using technology to create cool experiences, making people laugh, writing, and traveling.

Originally from New York City, I was fortunate enough to be exposed to a world of diverse passions, interests, and cusines at a young age and never looked back.

I use technology and comedy as tools to explore all the world has to offer. Currently I work at AT&T coming up with cool ways to innovate the standard retail experience, learning how to say y'all, and eating my weight in barbecue.

I'm excited to find my next adventure in the context of front-end development, the world of entertainment, and an exciting city!
